The Enchanting View of Oregon

Welcome to my website! This page was created from the memories I wish to share about my trip to Oregon. Please explore my page, and enjoy the feeling of being in Oregon yourself! To the right is a carousel of photos displaying some of my favorite places I went to in Oregon.

My trip to the First Starbucks

Near Portland, heading towards Seattle, is the first ever Starbucks Built. They have a tradition of sticking gum in an alley as a decoration.

The Beauty Hike

The waterfall is a picture of a hike I went on near the outskirts of Portland. I was surprised to find a waterfall as well!

The town of Portland

A few pictures depict the city of Portland and how close everything is to eachother. The community is much the same, and the locals were very nice.

Reflection of LA vs. Portland

Portland was peaceful compared to the hectic nature of LA. Everyone just seems to be in a good mood when the woods surround you.
